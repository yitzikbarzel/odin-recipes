Beetroot carpaccio

Beetroot carpaccio with fruit and cheese

Beet carpaccio, accompanied by cheese fruits and refreshing cilantro leaves

ingredients

  • Two medium cooked beets
  • 2 medium kiwi cut into cubes
  • 1 persimmon cut into cubes
  • 70 grams of Bulgarian cheese
  • A handful of coriander
  • 2 spoons of vinegar
  • 3 spoonse of olive oil
  • A spoonful of honey
  • 3 spoons of lemon juice
  • Half a teaspoon of thyme
  • Half a teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • Half a teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Slice the beets into thin slices
  2. Mix the sauce ingredients
  3. Pour over the flat beets
  4. Scatter the fruit around
  5. Crumble the cheese in the middle
  6. Chop the cilantro and spread it on the plate from above
