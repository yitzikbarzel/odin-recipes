Itzik's hummus

My special hummus dish

Middle Eastern Italian, a dish you will make again and again

ingredients

  • 400 grams of cooked hummus
  • A cup of tahini
  • Half a teaspoon of cumin
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Half a teaspoon of salt
  • 5 tablespoons of oil
  • Half a glass of warm water

Steps

  1. Grind the chickpeas
  2. Pour the cup of tahini
  3. mix
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients
  5. Grind well for 10 minutes with a minute rest every 2 minutes

Tip: serve warm alongside fresh pita
