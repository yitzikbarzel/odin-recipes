Arais

Three halves of Arais pita bread

Arais from veal with amazing flavors that combine spicy and sweet in one pita (or rather half)... a dish that will become your guilty pleasure

ingredients

  • 250 grams of medium fat veal
  • 4 pita halves
  • Half a chopped onion
  • Half a teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • Half a teaspoon of salt
  • A quarter cup of olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons teriyaki
  • A teaspoon of crushed garlic
  • A spoonful of chicken grill

Steps

  1. Chop the onion
  2. Mix well in a bowl with the meat
  3. Add the salt and pepper
  4. Put the oil, the grill spice and the garlic in a flat plate
  5. mix well
  6. Dip the pita well on both sides
  7. Fill it with the meat mixture
  8. Put on the grill for 3 minutes on each side
